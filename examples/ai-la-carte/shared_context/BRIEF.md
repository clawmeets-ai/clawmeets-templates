# Solopreneur Brief

## Who I Am

Solo founder with a technical background, building this as a bootstrapped MVP. I can build the app myself — I need this team to help me figure out **WHO** to build it for and **WHAT** to build first.

## What I Need From This Team

### 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Which user segment should I target first?
- Why that segment over others?
- How big is this segment? Where do they hang out online/offline?

### 2. MVP Scope
- What's the minimum feature set to validate with the ICP?
- What should I explicitly NOT build for v1?
- User stories for the first release
- What does "good enough" look like for each feature?

### 3. Go-To-Market Strategy
- How do I reach the ICP?
- What's the positioning and messaging?
- Pricing model (free, freemium, subscription, per-use?)
- Launch channels and tactics
- First 1,000 users plan

## Constraints

- **Solo developer** — 2-3 months to MVP
- **Budget**: <$5K for launch marketing
- **Revenue goal**: Validate willingness to pay within 90 days of launch
- **No team to scale**: Whatever I build, I need to maintain and support alone

## What Success Looks Like

A clear, opinionated plan I can execute on — not a list of options. I want the team to debate and converge on recommendations, with reasoning I can evaluate.

Specifically:
- One recommended ICP (not three "equally valid" segments)
- A feature list where everything is explicitly IN or OUT for v1
- A GTM plan with a primary channel and concrete first steps
- Pricing recommendation with rationale grounded in persona willingness to pay
